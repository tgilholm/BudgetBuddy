## Overview
BudgetBuddy is designed to overcome some of the shortcomings inherent in competing budget tracking apps. It aims for a seamless system for keeping track of one's monthly/weekly budget.

## Installation
Simply download to your device one of the available .apk files in the *Releases* section, navigate to the downloads directory in your files app, and click on the file. *Note- you may need to enable "Install unknown apps" in your Android Settings*. As of 06/02/2026, the application functions only on *Android* devices, with plans for iOS releases coming later.
